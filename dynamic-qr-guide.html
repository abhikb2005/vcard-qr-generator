1. What Are Dynamic QR Codes?

A Dynamic QR Code is a QR code whose destination content can be changed after it has been printed or shared. Unlike a static QR code where the data is fixed inside the code itself, a dynamic QR code points to a short redirect URL that you control.

This means you can:

  • Update contact details (name, phone, email, company) anytime without generating a new QR code
  • Track every scan with detailed analytics — see when, where, and how often your code is scanned
  • Keep the same printed QR code on business cards, stickers, flyers, and signage forever
Key Benefit: Print once, update forever. Change jobs, get a new number, or update your title — your QR code stays the same while the contact info it delivers is always current.

6. Understanding Scan Analytics

Every Dynamic QR code comes with built-in analytics. From your dashboard, you can see:

  • Total Scans: The total number of times your QR code has been scanned
  • Unique Scans: Deduplicated count (one per device within a 60-second window)
  • Country: Which countries your scans are coming from
  • Device Type: Mobile vs desktop breakdown
  • Referrer: Where the scan originated (camera app, browser, QR reader app)
  • Scan History: A timeline of scan activity
Privacy Note: We never store raw IP addresses. All IPs are hashed with SHA-256 before storage for GDPR compliance. Analytics cannot be used to identify individual scanners. See our Privacy Policy for details.

10. Troubleshooting

My QR code won't scan

  • Ensure the QR code is printed at least 1×1 inch (2.5 cm)
  • Use high contrast — dark code on a light background
  • Check that the surface isn't damaged, wrinkled, or obscured
  • Try scanning with a different phone or QR reader app

I scanned the code but it shows an error

  • Check that you have an active internet connection (required for Dynamic QR codes)
  • Verify that your subscription is still active
  • Make sure the QR code hasn't been deleted from your dashboard

I updated the contact info but the old details still show

  • Changes take effect immediately — try scanning again
  • If the old data persists, clear your phone's browser cache and rescan
  • Some QR reader apps cache results — try scanning with your phone's native camera app instead

My scan analytics aren't updating

  • Repeat scans from the same device within 60 seconds are deduplicated and counted as one scan
  • Analytics may take a few moments to appear in your dashboard
  • If the issue persists, contact us at contact@vcardqrcodegenerator.com

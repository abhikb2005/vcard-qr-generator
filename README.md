# vCard QR Code Generator

**Live site:** https://www.vcardqrcodegenerator.com/

**Dynamic QR Codes:** https://www.vcardqrcodegenerator.com/dynamic-qr-code-generator.html

Free, fast, and private QR code creation for digital business cards—everything runs in the browser so your contact data never leaves your device.

## Features
- Generate vCard QR codes instantly
- Create dynamic QR codes that can be edited after printing
- High error correction for reliable scanning
- Download ready-to-share PNG files
- Built-in dark mode for easy reading
- No data storage or server processing

## Dynamic QR Codes

The static generator is free and privacy-first for permanent contact details. The Dynamic QR dashboard is for business cards, flyers, badges, and campaigns where the QR code destination may need to change after printing.

- Dynamic app: https://app.vcardqrcodegenerator.com/login
- Editable QR guide: https://www.vcardqrcodegenerator.com/blog/editable-qr-code/

## Usage
Fill in your contact details, watch the QR code render in real time, then download or share the image anywhere you need it.

## Development
Edit `index.html` for UI changes, update assets in the `data/` and `templates/` folders, and open `index.html` locally in a browser to preview updates.

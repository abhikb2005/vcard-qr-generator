When dynamic QR codes are worth it

  • Business cards printed in bulk
  • Conference badges and event signs
  • Restaurant menus and table tents
  • Real estate flyers and property signs
  • Marketing campaigns where the landing page changes

Static QR codes are still the best choice when the information is permanent. A free static vCard QR code stores contact details directly inside the QR pattern, so it can work without a hosted profile. But printed assets often outlive the details printed on them. People change phone numbers, job titles, websites, calendars, and campaign pages.

A dynamic QR code solves that by making the QR pattern point to a stable short link. The short link can route to the newest contact profile or destination. That is the difference between reprinting every card and simply editing a dashboard.

What Is Scantrust?

Scantrust is a Swiss enterprise software platform founded in 2014 and headquartered in Lausanne, with offices in Amsterdam, Shanghai, Singapore, Bangkok, and Kyiv. The platform specializes in anti-counterfeiting, supply chain traceability, and connected packaging for global brands. Their core technology uses “Secure QR codes” — patented copy-detection patterns that can identify counterfeit products when scanned.

Scantrust’s QR codes are dynamic and server-dependent: every scan routes through their platform for authentication, analytics, and content delivery. The platform offers GS1 Digital Link compliance, Digital Product Passports (DPP) for EU regulations, dynamic landing pages, geo-based content redirection, and scan analytics. Their self-serve “Scantrust Pro” plan starts at $250/month for up to 50 QR codes, while the Pro+ plan costs $650/month for up to 100 codes. Enterprise pricing requires a custom quote. For someone who simply needs a vCard QR code for a business card, Scantrust is wildly overbuilt and overpriced.

  • Complexity overkill — Scantrust includes anti-counterfeiting, supply chain traceability, GS1 Digital Link, Digital Product Passports, and serialized code management. If you just need a QR code that saves your contact info when scanned, this complexity adds cost and friction with zero benefit.

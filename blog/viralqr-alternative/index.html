What Is ViralQR?

ViralQR is a QR code generator platform founded in 2022 and based in Cyprus. It offers dynamic QR codes with scan analytics, custom designs, password protection, scheduled campaigns, and a dedicated “vCard+” digital business card product. The platform supports 14 languages and targets businesses looking for trackable, editable QR codes with detailed engagement data.

For users who need a simple vCard QR code for their business card, ViralQR’s biggest issue is its pricing model: there is no free plan. The cheapest option is a $1.49 “7-day trial” that automatically renews at $39/month — a 26x price increase that’s easy to miss in the fine print.

Why Look for a ViralQR Alternative?

  • No free plan at all — Unlike most QR code generators, ViralQR offers no free tier. You can generate a basic static QR code on their site, but downloading, editing, or tracking requires a paid plan. For a single vCard QR code, paying $39/month is hard to justify.
  • Deceptive trial pricing — The $1.49 Basic Access and $1.99 Full Access plans look affordable, but they’re 7-day trials that auto-renew at $39/month. That’s $468/year for a QR code generator. The fine print states this clearly, but the headline pricing can be misleading.
  • Dynamic codes die with your subscription — ViralQR’s own FAQ states: “Your dynamic QR codes will not be active till the moment of subscription renewal.” If you cancel and have a vCard+ code on your printed business cards, those codes stop working immediately.
  • Server-side tracking — Every dynamic QR code scan is tracked with location (city-level), device type, operating system, and exact scan time. For privacy-conscious contact sharing, this data collection may not be acceptable.
  • No bulk generation — ViralQR’s own FAQ states that bulk QR code creation is “under development.” If you need QR codes for a team, you have to create them one at a time.
  • No API — The API is also “under development,” limiting integration options for technical users.

What Is Unitag?

Unitag (unitag.io) is a French-British QR code solutions company founded in 2013 that positions itself as a leader in corporate QR code solutions. Based in Toulouse, France and London, UK, Unitag serves enterprise clients including Chanel, Warner Bros, Alibaba, and Orangina. The platform offers dynamic QR codes, advanced scan analytics with geolocation, campaign management, batch creation (Atlas), custom domain names, and digital business cards (vCard Pro).

Unitag supports 11 types of QR codes including URLs, text, email, phone, SMS, Wi-Fi, location, events, and vCards. Their free tier creates low-resolution static QR codes limited to 100 scans. Paid plans start at $29/month (Silver) and go up to $439+/month (Diamond) for enterprise features like SSO, packaging solutions, and GS1 certification. Digital business cards (vCard Pro) are an add-on priced per card. While Unitag is a powerful enterprise platform, it’s overkill and expensive for users who simply need a vCard QR code for their business card or resume.

Why Look for a Unitag Alternative?

  • Expensive subscriptions — Unitag’s cheapest paid plan is $29/month (Silver), and useful analytics require the $59/month Gold plan or higher. For a simple vCard QR code, paying $348–$708/year is hard to justify.
  • vCard Pro costs extra per card — Unitag’s digital business cards (vCard Pro) are an add-on module priced at $3.05/month per card. A team of 20 people would cost $61/month just for the cards, on top of the base subscription. For privacy-conscious users, this also means contact data lives on Unitag’s servers.
  • Free tier is severely limited — Unitag’s free QR codes are low-resolution and capped at 100 scans. Some users report the free generator only produces sample codes that aren’t usable. SVG downloads require a paid upgrade.
  • Server-side data processing — Unitag generates and hosts all QR codes and digital business cards on their cloud servers. Your contact data is stored on their infrastructure, with analytics tracking every scan’s location and device.
  • Dashboard in French only for vCards — Unitag’s own documentation notes that the vCard creation dashboard is “available in French only,” which can be challenging for non-French speakers.
  • Enterprise complexity — Unitag is designed for large organizations managing thousands of QR codes across campaigns, with features like sub-organizations, packaging solutions, and SKU management. This complexity is unnecessary if you just need a contact QR code.

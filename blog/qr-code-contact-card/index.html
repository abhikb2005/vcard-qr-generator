Essential Design and Testing Steps

After generating your QR code for contact details, download the high-resolution PNG. Test on multiple smartphone models and QR scanning apps to ensure all vCard fields import correctly. Maintain a white quiet zone and high contrast for reliable scanning.

  • Use at least 2 x 2 cm for print and 100 x 100 px for digital use.
  • Verify scanning performance under varied lighting conditions.
  • Check that all contact fields populate accurately after scanning.

Deployment and Maintenance Best Practices

Embed your QR code contact card in email signatures, digital brochures, or printed materials. Export at 300 DPI for crisp print quality and avoid stretching the image. When contact details change, regenerate the code and replace it across all channels promptly to maintain consistency.

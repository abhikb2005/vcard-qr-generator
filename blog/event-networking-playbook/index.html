Event Networking Playbook with QR Codes

Prepare for conferences with a QR-powered workflow for effortless introductions.

You only have a few minutes with each person at a conference. A polished QR workflow lets you capture details and make a professional impression without slowing things down.

Pre-event preparation

Research the attendee list and tailor your contact card to the industries you want to reach. Mention a relevant case study or offer in the notes field.

Pack backup batteries and a small display stand so the QR code stays visible even during long expo days.

  • Print signage that matches your booth or outfit
  • Queue a welcome email that triggers after each scan
  • Plan conversation starters around current industry news

On-site execution

Keep the QR code within reach for quick scanning. If the event is crowded, enlarge it on a tablet so people can scan while they wait.

Rotate conversation roles with teammates so someone always monitors who has scanned and who still needs a nudge.

  • Log every interaction in a shared doc or CRM
  • Offer a takeaway such as a downloadable guide
  • Collect feedback on messaging in real time

Post-event follow-up

Send a recap email that includes your QR code again along with a short list of resources mentioned at the booth.

Schedule personal calls with the strongest prospects and reference the details captured during the event.

  • Categorize contacts by urgency and interest
  • Track conversions tied to each event in your CRM
  • Invite new connections to a webinar or community group

Why People Look for Free QR Code Generator Alternatives

Free QR Code Generator (free-qr-code-generator.com) is a Singapore-based online tool that creates static QR codes for URLs, Wi-Fi, vCard, text, social media, and more. It’s genuinely free for basic static codes.

But professionals who need vCard QR codes for business cards and networking run into limitations:

  • Generic tool, not vCard-focused — vCard is just one of many QR types. The tool doesn’t offer vCard-specific features like field validation, format testing, or phone compatibility checks.
  • No bulk generation — Need QR codes for an entire team? You’d have to create them one at a time. No CSV upload, no batch processing.
  • Limited customization — Basic color options at best. No logo overlay, no corner style options, no frame templates for professional branding.
  • Server-side processing — Your contact details are sent to their servers to generate the code. Privacy-conscious users want everything to happen in the browser.
  • No download format options — Limited export formats compared to tools that offer PNG, SVG, and high-resolution downloads.

Free QR Code Generator works fine for quick URL codes. But for dedicated vCard contact sharing—especially at scale—a purpose-built tool saves time and produces better results.

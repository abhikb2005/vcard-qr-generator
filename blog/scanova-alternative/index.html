Why People Look for Scanova Alternatives

Scanova has been in the QR code space for years. It offers dynamic QR codes, scan analytics dashboards, and enterprise-level management tools. For marketing teams running large-scale campaigns across multiple channels, those features deliver genuine value.

But many professionals searching for a vCard QR code solution run into friction with Scanova:

  • Pricing starts at ~$5/month — Even the basic plan has a monthly cost. If you need a handful of static vCard codes for business cards, paying monthly for a QR code feels wrong.
  • Scan limits on lower plans — Scanova caps the number of scans per month on cheaper tiers. Static vCard QR codes should have no scan limits since they don’t use any server resources.
  • Account creation required — You can’t generate anything without signing up, verifying your email, and setting up a workspace. That’s a lot of friction for a one-minute task.
  • Data stored on their servers — Scanova is a cloud platform. Your contact details (phone numbers, emails, addresses) are processed and stored on their infrastructure. For privacy-conscious users, that’s a concern.
  • Complex interface — Scanova supports dozens of QR code types, campaign management, and team roles. If you just want a vCard code, the interface has more buttons than you need.

Scanova is built for enterprise QR code management. If your need is simpler—create a contact-sharing QR code and download it—a focused, free tool is the better fit.

To be fair, Scanova is the better tool in specific scenarios:

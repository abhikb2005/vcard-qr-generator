What Is Trueqrcode?

Trueqrcode is a Netherlands-based QR code management platform founded in 2022. It offers dynamic QR codes, scan analytics with GPS tracking, customizable designs with logos and templates, bulk creation via Excel upload, and support for QR types including website URLs, PDFs, vCards, WiFi, multi-links, and GS1 Digital Link codes.

Trueqrcode positions itself as a business-grade QR management system used by over 30,000 customers. But for individuals and small businesses who simply need a vCard QR code for their business card, the subscription cost and dynamic code dependency are hard to justify.

Why Look for a Trueqrcode Alternative?

  • No free plan — Trueqrcode offers only a 10-day free trial. After that, you must pay. Even the cheapest Basic plan ($5.95/month billed annually) limits you to just 2 QR codes and 250 monthly scans. For a single vCard QR code on your business card, that’s an unnecessary recurring expense.
  • QR codes expire when you cancel — Trueqrcode uses dynamic QR codes that route through their servers. Cancel your subscription, and every QR code you’ve printed stops working. If your vCard code is on 1,000 business cards, they all become useless.
  • Strict scan limits — The Basic plan caps you at 250 scans per month. The Pro plan at 1,500. Only the Business plan ($49.95/month) offers unlimited scans. If your QR code goes viral at a conference, you could hit your limit mid-event.
  • Annual billing required — Trueqrcode’s advertised prices assume annual billing. Quarterly billing is available but costs more. There is no month-to-month option, so you’re committing to at least three months upfront.
  • Privacy concerns — Dynamic QR codes route every scan through Trueqrcode’s servers, tracking device type, location, GPS coordinates, and time of day. For privacy-sensitive contact sharing, this may not be acceptable.
  • Overkill for vCard use cases — Trueqrcode is built for managing large QR code campaigns across teams. If you just need a vCard QR code that saves your contact info when scanned, you don’t need folders, templates, GPS tracking, or team accounts.

  • General-purpose tool — With 13+ QR code types, the interface isn’t optimized specifically for vCard creation. Finding and configuring the right vCard fields takes more clicks than a purpose-built tool.

free-qr-code-generator.com works fine for quick one-off URL QR codes. But for a dedicated, truly free vCard QR code, a purpose-built generator offers a smoother experience.

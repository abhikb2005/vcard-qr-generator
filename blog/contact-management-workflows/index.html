Contact Management Workflows After a QR Code Scan

Organize new connections captured via QR codes and vCards.

A great QR experience continues in your CRM. Put a structure in place so every scan translates into a meaningful relationship.

Centralize new contacts

Create a dedicated inbox or automation that collects every vCard submission in one queue. Consistency prevents leads from slipping through the cracks.

Sync the queue with your CRM and map each field to the appropriate property so downstream workflows stay clean.

  • Use naming conventions that identify the event or campaign
  • Validate data before it enters your main CRM records
  • Log the QR code version that generated each contact

Score and prioritize

Assign simple lead scores based on job title, company size, or expressed interest. This helps teams focus follow-ups on the right people.

Automate task creation when a scan meets your priority criteria. Speed matters when intent is high.

  • Set thresholds that trigger human outreach
  • Integrate notifications with the tools your team already uses
  • Review scoring models monthly and adjust as patterns emerge

Track long-term impact

Measure revenue and retention metrics tied to QR-generated contacts. Seeing the full journey validates your networking investments.

Document stories from successful connections and share them internally. They make the case for continuing QR-powered programs.

  • Build dashboards that visualize conversions from QR scans
  • Compare performance across events or campaigns
  • Use insights to refine your messaging for the next outing

After You Generate Your QR Code

Once you've downloaded your vCard QR code, test it thoroughly before distributing. Scan with multiple devices—both iPhone and Android—to confirm universal compatibility. When printing on business cards or marketing materials, maintain a minimum size of 2cm × 2cm and ensure adequate quiet zone (white space) around the code for reliable scanning. For digital use, add the QR code to your email signature, LinkedIn profile, or personal website. If you're embedding in PDFs or presentations, use the original PNG file without resizing to preserve scan quality. Keep your master PNG file saved safely—if your contact details change, you'll need to generate a new code and replace it everywhere you've used the old one.

What Is Uniqode?

Uniqode (formerly Beaconstac) is an enterprise QR code management platform used by over 50,000 businesses worldwide. It offers dynamic QR codes, digital business cards, scan analytics, team management with role-based access, and integrations with tools like Salesforce, Google Analytics, and Zapier.

Uniqode is a serious platform for large-scale QR code campaigns. But for individuals and small businesses who need a simple vCard QR code for their business card, Uniqode’s complexity and cost are hard to justify.

Why Look for a Uniqode Alternative?

  • Expensive subscriptions — Uniqode starts at $9/month (billed annually at $108/year) for just 50 dynamic QR codes. The Core plan is $49/month ($588/year), and Plus is $99/month ($1,188/year). For a single vCard QR code on your business card, that’s a lot of money.
  • QR codes expire when you cancel — This is the biggest risk. Uniqode uses dynamic QR codes that route through their servers. Cancel your subscription, and every QR code you’ve printed on business cards, flyers, and marketing materials stops working. Multiple Trustpilot reviewers report being blindsided by this.
  • Scan limits — Even on paid plans, Uniqode imposes annual scan limits (500K on Essential, 1M on Core, 2M on Plus). Exceed the limit and your QR codes become inactive until you upgrade.
  • Annual-only billing — Uniqode doesn’t offer monthly plans. You’re locked into a full year commitment upfront.
  • Feature downgrades after acquisition — After Beaconstac rebranded to Uniqode, multiple users reported losing features they previously had access to, with prices increasing significantly.
  • Privacy concerns — Dynamic QR codes route every scan through Uniqode’s servers. Your contacts’ devices, locations, and scan times are tracked. For privacy-sensitive contact sharing, this may not be acceptable.

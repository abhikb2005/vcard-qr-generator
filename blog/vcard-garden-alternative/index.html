Why People Look for vCard Garden Alternatives

vCard Garden (vcardgarden.com) is a hosted digital business card platform aimed at realtors, contractors, consultants, and freelancers. You fill out a form, pick a template, and get a professional page at vcardgarden.com/your-name with a QR code and one-tap contact download. It’s polished and simple.

But many professionals hit friction when they dig into the details:

  • Monthly subscription required — There is no free plan. After a 14-day trial, vCard Garden costs $9/month (Starter), $19/month (Pro), or $29/month (Business). For a single vCard QR code, that’s $108–$348 per year.
  • Platform dependency — Your page lives on vCard Garden’s servers at their domain. If you cancel your subscription or the service shuts down, your QR codes stop working. Every business card, yard sign, or flyer you printed becomes useless.
  • Page limits per plan — The Starter plan caps you at 5 pages, Pro at 25, and Business at 50. For teams or events that need hundreds of QR codes, this doesn’t scale.
  • No bulk CSV generation — vCard Garden creates pages one at a time through its form. There’s no way to upload a spreadsheet and generate QR codes in batch for a whole team or event.
  • Server-stored data — Your contact information is hosted on vCard Garden’s servers. Privacy-conscious users may prefer tools that process data entirely in the browser.
  • Limited QR code customization — vCard Garden provides a basic QR code for your page. You can’t customize QR code colors, corner styles, or embed a logo directly into the code itself.

vCard Garden is a well-designed service for professionals who want a hosted landing page. But if you just need a free QR code for your business card without monthly fees or platform lock-in, a standalone generator is the better fit.

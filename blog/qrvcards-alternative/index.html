Why People Look for QRvCards Alternatives

QRvCards (qrvcards.in) is an India-based SaaS platform that lets you build digital business cards and mini-websites with 21+ templates, custom QR codes, and a user management dashboard. It targets small businesses and teams who want a hosted digital identity.

But professionals looking for a simple, free vCard QR code often run into friction:

  • Account required — QRvCards requires registration before you can create anything. If you just need a quick QR code for your business card, creating an account feels like overkill.
  • Paid plans for full features — The free plan is limited in storage and disables features like ChatGPT integration. Paid plans start at ₹499/year (~$6) for small businesses and go up to ₹15,000 for a lifetime distributor license. For a simple vCard QR code, that’s unnecessary cost.
  • Server-stored data — Your contact information is stored on QRvCards’ cloud servers. For privacy-conscious users, this is a concern. Your data is managed by a third party rather than staying on your device.
  • Platform lock-in — QRvCards generates dynamic QR codes that point to their hosted pages. If the service goes down or you stop paying, your QR codes stop working. Static vCard QR codes work forever because the data is encoded directly in the code.
  • No bulk CSV generation — QRvCards focuses on individual card creation through its dashboard. There’s no way to upload a CSV and generate hundreds of QR codes at once for a team or event.
  • Limited QR code customization on free plan — While QRvCards offers some QR code customization, the free tier is restrictive. You need a paid plan for full design control.

QRvCards is a solid platform if you want a hosted digital business card with templates and team management. But if you need a fast, free, private vCard QR code — no account, no subscription, no server storage — a dedicated generator is the better fit.

Creating Your vCard QR Code

Begin by accessing our user-friendly generator on the homepage. Input your accurate contact information and click Generate to produce your personalized vCard QR code instantly.

Exporting Your QR Code

When exporting your vCard QR code for email signatures, presentations, or PDFs, opt for high-resolution PNG files. Always maintain the original square aspect ratio to prevent distortion and scanning issues.

For print materials like brochures, consider exporting vector or SVG formats for scalability without quality loss. This ensures your QR code remains crisp on all sizes of printed media.

Embedding and Sharing Best Practices

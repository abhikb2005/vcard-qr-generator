How to Create and Use vCard QR Codes

Step-by-step guide to generating, testing, and embedding your own vCard QR codes for efficient networking and contact sharing.

Learn More

Creating Your vCard QR Code

Start by opening our QR code generator on the homepage. Input your contact details such as name, phone number, and email, then click “Generate” to quickly produce your personal vCard QR code.

Before sharing or printing, test your QR code on multiple devices and apps to ensure compatibility. Make sure to leave a clear white quiet zone around the code and use high-contrast colors to facilitate easy scanning.

Embedding and Sharing Best Practices

Export your vCard QR code as a high-resolution PNG, preferably at a size suitable for your application, without stretching or distorting the image. Incorporate it into email signatures, presentations, business cards, or marketing material to maximize visibility.

Keep your QR code updated by regenerating it if your contact information changes, then replace older versions everywhere it appears to maintain consistent and professional networking experiences.

What Was Compzets QR Code Generator?

Compzets QR Code Generator was a free online tool by compzets.com that let users generate QR codes for URLs, phone numbers, SMS messages, and email addresses. It also provided a permalink for each QR code so you could share it easily. The tool was web-based, proprietary, and offered at no cost.

However, Compzets has been discontinued. The website is no longer available, and users who relied on it need to find a replacement. If you were using Compzets for basic QR codes — or if you need a more capable tool for creating vCard QR codes — our free generator is a strong alternative.

Why You Need a Compzets Alternative

Even when Compzets was active, it had significant limitations compared to a purpose-built vCard QR code tool:

  • No vCard support — Compzets generated QR codes for URLs, phone numbers, SMS, and email, but it had no dedicated vCard fields. Creating a proper contact QR code with name, title, company, and address wasn’t possible.
  • No customization — The tool produced basic black-and-white QR codes with no custom colors, corner styles, or logo options. For business cards, a branded QR code makes a real difference.
  • No bulk generation — Users could only create one QR code at a time. There was no CSV upload or batch processing for teams and events.
  • No SVG downloads — Without vector output, QR codes from Compzets could pixelate when printed at larger sizes on business cards and marketing materials.
  • Now offline — The most critical issue: Compzets is discontinued and inaccessible. Any QR codes that relied on Compzets permalinks are now broken.

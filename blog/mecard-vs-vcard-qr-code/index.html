MeCard vs vCard QR Codes: Which Should You Use?

When creating a QR code for your contact information, you're often faced with a choice: vCard or MeCard. Both formats allow phones to save your details instantly, but they differ significantly in features, compatibility, and data capacity. In this guide, we'll break down the differences and help you choose the right format for your digital business card.

Definitions: vCard vs MeCard

What is vCard?

vCard (Virtual Contact File) is the global standard for electronic business cards. It is a rich format capable of storing extensive information, including profile photos, multiple phone numbers, social media links, and even birthdays. It is the default format used by Apple Contacts, Google Contacts, and Outlook.

What is MeCard?

MeCard is a simplified contact file format originally developed by NTT DoCoMo for use in Japan. It is designed to be compact and lightweight, using less data than vCard. This results in a simpler QR code pattern that is easier to scan, but at the cost of supporting fewer data fields.

Pros & Cons of vCard

✅ Pros

  • Supports extensive contact details
  • Allows for profile images and logos
  • Professional standard for business
  • Seamless iOS and Android integration

❌ Cons

  • QR code pattern can become complex/dense
  • Requires higher print quality for scanning

Pros & Cons of MeCard

✅ Pros

  • Very compact QR code pattern
  • Scans easily even at small sizes
  • Good for basic info (Name + Phone)

❌ Cons

  • Very limited fields (no photo, minimal address)
  • Details often get merged incorrectly
  • Outdated standard

Smartphone Compatibility

Both formats are widely readable by modern smartphones.

  • iPhone (iOS): The native camera app handles vCard perfectly, displaying a rich contact card layout. It also scans MeCard, but the experience is often less polished.
  • Android: Google Lens and native cameras support both. vCard is preferred for full integration with Google Contacts.

For a professional audience, vCard ensures your job title, company name, and website are saved correctly.

Our Recommendation

Choose vCard for Business

For 99% of users, vCard is the better choice. It looks professional, saves all your important business details, and works flawlessly on modern devices.

Use MeCard ONLY if:

  • You are printing a very tiny QR code (less than 1 inch)
  • You only need to share a name and phone number

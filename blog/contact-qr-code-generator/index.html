Best Practices for Contact QR Codes

  • Maintain Quiet Zone: Keep a white space around the code to aid scanning.
  • Size Matters: Use at least 2 x 2 cm for printed materials to ensure readability.
  • Test Widely: Verify scans on iOS and Android devices with default camera apps.
  • Use High Resolution: Export at 300 DPI for print and 600×600 px for digital use to avoid pixelation.

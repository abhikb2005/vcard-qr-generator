Why People Look for SaferCodes Alternatives

SaferCodes (safer.codes) launched as a “COVID bundle for small businesses”—a collection of 10 QR code tools designed for restaurants, bars, and local shops. Its core features include customer check-in, digital menus, WiFi sharing, Google review collection, and newsletter integration with Mailchimp.

While SaferCodes does offer a vCard/contact QR type, it’s not the platform’s primary focus. Professionals searching for a vCard QR code solution run into friction:

  • COVID-era tool, not a contact tool — SaferCodes was designed for check-ins, menus, and WiFi sharing. vCard is a secondary feature buried among restaurant-focused tools.
  • Monthly subscription pricing — Paid plans start at $4/month ($48/year). Simple vCard codes shouldn’t cost a recurring fee.
  • Account required — You must create an account before generating any codes. For a quick one-off task, that’s unnecessary friction.
  • Cloud-based with data purging — SaferCodes stores customer data on its servers and purges visitor data after 30 days. Privacy-conscious users want browser-based generation with no data retention.
  • Feature overload — Facebook Pixel integration, Google Analytics tracking, retargeting, mailing list growth—useful for restaurant marketing, but overkill for sharing your contact details on a business card.

SaferCodes serves local businesses needing contactless operations tools. For dedicated vCard contact sharing, a free, purpose-built tool is the more efficient choice.

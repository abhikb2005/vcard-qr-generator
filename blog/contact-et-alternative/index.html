Why People Look for Contact.et Alternatives

Contact.et is a simple, free digital business card maker. You enter your name, email, phone, and address, and it generates a QR code and downloadable VCF file. It requires no signup and works in the browser—similar to our tool in philosophy.

But professionals looking for more control over their vCard QR codes often hit limitations:

  • No QR code customization — Contact.et generates a plain black-and-white QR code. No custom colors, no corner styles, no background options. For branded business cards, design matters.
  • No logo support — You cannot embed a company logo or photo into the QR code. This is a common need for professional and corporate use.
  • No bulk generation — Contact.et creates one card at a time. Teams, event organizers, and HR departments need to generate dozens or hundreds of vCard QR codes from a spreadsheet.
  • Limited download options — The tool focuses on VCF file downloads and QR code images, but doesn’t offer high-resolution SVG exports or print-ready formats.
  • Digital card focus vs. QR code focus — Contact.et is designed as a digital business card preview tool. If you specifically need a QR code for your business card, a purpose-built QR generator gives you more options.
  • Smaller tool, less community — Contact.et is a relatively small, niche tool. Documentation, updates, and community support are limited compared to established QR code generators.

Contact.et does what it promises—free, simple business card creation. But if you need customization, bulk features, or print-ready QR codes, a dedicated vCard QR code generator is the better choice.

Why People Look for Yohn.io Alternatives

Yohn.io (formerly Colibri) is a German company based in Baden-Württemberg that positions itself as a three-in-one platform: digital business cards, QR code generator, and branded URL shortener. It runs on AWS infrastructure across 220+ global servers and emphasizes GDPR compliance—important for European businesses.

For enterprise teams that need Apple Wallet integration, Google Workspace syncing, and whitelabel dashboards, Yohn.io is a solid choice. But for individuals and small businesses who just need a vCard QR code, there are some real pain points:

  • Free tier requires your email — Even the free static QR code tools on Yohn.io require you to enter your email address. Instead of downloading directly, your QR code is sent via email. For a simple QR code, that’s an unnecessary friction point.
  • Pricing adds up quickly — A single digital business card costs $10/year. The Yohn+ plan with dynamic QR codes, analytics, and custom domains costs $29/month after a 7-day trial. Corporate and Enterprise tiers require contacting sales. For a freelancer who just needs one vCard QR code, this is significant overkill.
  • Your data lives on their servers — Yohn.io’s digital business cards are hosted landing pages served from AWS. Your contact details—phone, email, address—are stored on their infrastructure. While they emphasize GDPR compliance, the data still lives on third-party servers.
  • Built for teams, not individuals — Features like SSO, Google Workspace import, whitelabel control panels, and multi-seat management are enterprise features. If you’re a solo professional or a small team, you’re paying for complexity you’ll never use.
  • Limited vCards on lower tiers — The Yohn+ plan caps you at 10 vCards. Only Corporate and Enterprise tiers offer unlimited vCards—and those require contacting sales for pricing.

Yohn.io is a legitimate platform with real value for enterprise teams. But if your primary need is creating QR codes for business cards, there’s a simpler, free way to do it.

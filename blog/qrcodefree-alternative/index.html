What Happened to QRCodeFree?

QRCodeFree.com was a free QR code generator that allowed users to create basic QR codes without cost. However, the site is no longer operational—as of 2026, the domain is parked on a domain marketplace and listed for sale at $895.

This is a common pattern with free QR code tools: they launch with a free model, struggle to monetize, and eventually shut down. When that happens, users lose access to any dynamic codes they created, and need to find a replacement.

If you were a QRCodeFree user or found it in a recommendation list, you need a reliable alternative. For vCard QR codes specifically, here’s what to look for.

What to Look for in a Free QR Code Generator

After QRCodeFree’s shutdown, choosing a replacement wisely matters. Here are the key criteria:

  • Actually free — Many “free” generators hit you with paywalls for basic features like downloading without watermarks, removing ads, or creating more than 1–2 codes.
  • No account required — If a free tool requires signup, it’s collecting your data. The best free tools let you generate and download without creating an account.
  • Privacy-first — Free tools that upload your data to their servers may use it for advertising or analytics. Browser-based generation keeps your information private.
  • Static codes that never expire — Dynamic codes depend on the provider’s servers. If the provider shuts down (like QRCodeFree did), your codes stop working. Static vCard codes work forever.
  • Purpose-built for your use case — Generic QR generators create many code types but often do vCard formatting poorly. A dedicated vCard tool ensures proper contact card formatting.

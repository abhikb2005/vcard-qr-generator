5 Mistakes Keeping You From Your Next Promotion

(And how to fix them this quarter)

You've been at the same level for 2+ years. Your reviews are "good" but not "great." You're doing the work, but not getting recognized. Sound familiar?

1

You're Solving Problems, Not Creating Solutions

The mistake: You wait for problems to arise, then fix them efficiently.

The fix: Anticipate problems before they happen. Propose systemic improvements. Document your proactive initiatives and their impact.

2

Your Wins Are Invisible

The mistake: You assume good work speaks for itself. It doesn't.

The fix: Create a "wins doc" updated bi-weekly. Share in team meetings. Quantify everything. Make your manager's promotion-case-for-you effortless to write.

Pick ONE mistake from above. Commit to fixing it over the next 30 days. Track your progress in a shared doc.

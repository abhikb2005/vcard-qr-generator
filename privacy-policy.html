Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 12, 2025

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how vCard QR Code Generator ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

vCard Information: All contact information you enter into the generator (name, phone, email, etc.) is processed exclusively within your web browser. This data is never sent to, stored on, or logged by our servers. Your contact information remains private to you.

Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous traffic data to understand how our visitors use the website. This includes information like page views, session duration, and browser type. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. Payments

When you purchase a Pro feature, your payment is handled directly by our payment processor, Dodo Payments. We do not collect, see, or store your credit card details. Please refer to Dodo Payments' privacy policy for information on how they handle your payment data.

3. Cookies & Consent

We use cookies for analytics (Google Analytics) and advertising (Google AdSense). When you first visit our site, a consent banner asks whether you accept or reject non-essential cookies. Your choice is saved in your browser's local storage.

  • If you accept: Analytics and advertising cookies are enabled.
  • If you reject: No analytics or advertising cookies are set. Only essential functionality is available.

You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or site data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense — display advertising
  • Simple Analytics — privacy-first analytics (no cookies)
  • Dodo Payments — payment processing

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@vcardqrcodegenerator.com.

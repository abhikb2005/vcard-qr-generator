Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous traffic data to understand how our visitors use the website. This includes information like page views, session duration, and browser type. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

When you purchase a Pro feature, your payment is handled directly by our payment processor, Stripe. We do not collect, see, or store your credit card details. Please refer to Stripe's privacy policy for information on how they handle your payment data.

3. Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies for essential website functionality and for our analytics service (Google Analytics). You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics for website analytics. This service has its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@vcardqrcodegenerator.com.

How to Use the vCard QR Code Generator

This guide walks you through creating your own digital business card QR code quickly and easily.

Steps:

  • Fill in your contact details in the form fields: Full Name, Phone Number, Email Address, Company, and Job Title.
  • Click the Fill Sample Data button to see example data pre-filled (optional).
  • After entering your details, click the Generate QR Code button.
  • Your personalized QR code will appear below the form.
  • You can download the QR code as an image using the Download QR Code button.
  • Use this QR code on business cards, email signatures, or anywhere you want to share your contact info.
